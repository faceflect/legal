# FaceFlect Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 1, 2026

Thank you for using FaceFlect! Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application ("FaceFlect").

## 1. Information We Collect

FaceFlect is designed to process and store your data **locally on your device**. We prioritize your privacy through completely offline, on-device processing.

- **Facial Data and Biometrics:** FaceFlect uses your device's camera to detect and recognize faces for the "Aura Scanner" and profile enrollment features. **All facial recognition data and biometric representations (mathematical embeddings) are encrypted and stored exclusively on your local device.** These embeddings are generated using a local on-device machine learning model and do not contain recognizable image data.
- **User-Provided Content:** Profile names, life journey details, relationship connections, tags, and dates you enter to build your family tree.
- **Images/Photos:** Photos you capture or select from your gallery for user profiles.

## 2. Permissions We Request

To provide our core features, FaceFlect requests the following Android permissions:

- **Camera:** Required to capture photos for enrolling profiles, verifying identity, and using the real-time Aura Scanner for face recognition.
- **Storage / Photos / Media:** Required to select profile pictures from your gallery and to import or export your encrypted family tree backups.
- **Biometric Authentication:** Used optionally to secure your account and backups natively on your device.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used strictly to provide the features of FaceFlect:

- To map and visualize your family tree and relationships.
- To enable face recognition and the Aura Scanner to identify enrolled family and friends.
- To generate "Relationship Insights" based on logged interactions.
- To facilitate secure on-device biometric login.

## 4. Data Sharing and Transfer

**We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data, facial data, or family tree information with third parties.** 

- **Local Storage:** By default, all data remains entirely on your device and is not transmitted to our servers or any cloud services.
- **User-Initiated Export:** You have the option to export your data (e.g., via email or personal cloud storage). This export is fully controlled by you, and you can secure it with a passcode. FaceFlect is not responsible for the security of data once exported by the user.

## 5. Third-Party Services

FaceFlect utilizes third-party tools for processing that operate locally on your device:
- **Google ML Kit:** Used locally for face detection. Facial data processed by ML Kit is used only to locate faces in the camera frame and is not transmitted off the device.
- **On-Device TFLite Model:** Used to generate encrypted mathematical representations (embeddings) from detected faces. This processing happens entirely within the app's local sandbox.
- **Android Biometric Prompt:** Used for securely authenticating the account owner locally.

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained only as long as you keep it on your device.
- You can delete individual profiles at any time within the app.
- You can delete your entire account and all associated data via the app settings. Doing so securely erases all related face data and application data from your local storage.
- If you uninstall FaceFlect, your local data will be deleted by the operating system.

## 7. Children's Privacy

FaceFlect allows you to add profiles for family members, including children. However, the app itself is not directed at children under the age of 13. Given that all data processing is local and controlled by the device owner, it is the account owner's responsibility to manage profiles reflecting children's data.

## 8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
